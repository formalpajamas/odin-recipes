Salmon Bowl Recipe

Description

One of my go-to recipes, spicy salmon on top of a bed of jasmine rice with carrots, cucumber, and avocado mixed in.

Ingredients

  • 2 Salmon Filets
  • 1/3 c. Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 c. Chili Garlic Sauce
  • Juice of one Lime
  • 2 tbs. Honey
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/3 c. Olive Oil
  • 1 c. Jasmine Rice
  • 1 Cucumber, sliced
  • 1 Avocado, sliced
  • 1 Carrot, cut into matchsticks

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 F and line baking sheet with foil. In a medium bowl whisk together soy sauce, chili garlic sauce, lime juice, honey, garlic, and olive oil. Add salmon and marinate while oven is preheating.
  2. Once oven is ready, place the salmon and marinade on the baking sheet and cook 20-25 minutes, until an internal temperature of 125 F.
  3. While salmon is cooking prepare rice in a rice cooker and chop veggies.
  4. Once salmon is done remove from oven and assemble the bowl. Rice on the bottom with the salmon and veggies on top.
