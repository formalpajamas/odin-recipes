Banana Oatmeal

Description

An easy morning breakfast option that should keep you full until lunch, we mix oatmeal with bananas, almonds, and peanut butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 serving of Oatmeal
  • 1 Banana, sliced
  • 1/4 c. Sliced Almonds
  • 1-2 tbs. Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Cook oatmeal per package instructions, mixed with water and a dash of salt.
  2. Slice banana and mix into the oatmeal along with the sliced almonds and peanut butter.
  3. Enjoy!
