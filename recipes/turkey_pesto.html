Turkey Pesto Sandwich

Description

A quick lunch using sourdough bread and pre-made pesto this comes together in a few minutes and has a satisfying mix of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Turkey deli meat
  • 1 tbs. Pesto
  • 1 tbs. Mayonnaise
  • Peperoncinos

Steps

  1. Toast sourdough bread.
  2. Once bread is ready apply pesto to one piece and mayonnaise to the other.
  3. Add turkey and peperoncinos, amount is flexible so whatever is to your liking.
  4. Other toppings you could consider adding - bacon, arugula, tomato, etc.
